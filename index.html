Frequently Asked Questions

How does Parallax AI work?
Parallax AI sends your query to multiple AI models simultaneously and displays their responses side-by-side. You only pay for the models you use, with transparent per-query pricing. All data is encrypted and processed securely.
Which AI models do you support?
We support 15 specialized AI models including Analytical AI, Creative AI, Technical AI, Business AI, Scientific AI, Empathetic AI, Educational AI, Conversational AI, and more. Each model is optimized for specific tasks and use cases.
How much does it cost?
We offer a free tier with 100 queries per month. Our pay-as-you-go plan starts at $0.001 per query with access to all 15 models. Pricing is transparent with real-time cost tracking, and you only pay for what you use.
Is my data private and secure?
Yes. All data is encrypted in transit and at rest. We never store your queries or responses beyond your session unless you explicitly save them. We comply with GDPR, SOC 2, and industry security standards.
Can I use my own API keys?
Yes! You can bring your own API keys for supported models. This gives you direct control over costs and usage while still benefiting from our comparison interface and synthesis features.
Can I export or share conversations?
Absolutely. Export conversations in Markdown or JSON format. Share comparisons with teammates via secure links. Create team workspaces for collaboration with full privacy controls.
What's the difference between synthesis and comparison?
Comparison shows all model responses side-by-side for manual review. Synthesis uses AI to automatically combine the best insights from all responses into a single comprehensive answer, citing which models contributed what.
Is there a free trial?
Yes! Our free tier includes 100 queries per month with access to 6 core models. No credit card required. You can upgrade to pay-as-you-go anytime to unlock all 15 models and advanced features.
